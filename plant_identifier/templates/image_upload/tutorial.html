BeeGPT Tutorial

Here's a basic tutorial on how to Identify a Plant

Step 1: Navigate to the right section on the home page

The "Identify a Plant" feature can be found on the home page. Simply scroll down and locate the "Try BeeGPT Now!" section.

Step 2: Click on the "Choose File" Button

Click on the "Choose File" button to select an image.

Step 3: Select your plant image

From the pop-up dialog, select your plant image that you would like to identify.

Step 4: Click on the "Upload" button

Click on the "Upload" button to learn about the plant form the image you have selected. It may take up to 10 seconds to process the image and provide information about its biodiversity.
